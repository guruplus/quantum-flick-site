About SAND

Exploring the nature of reality through science and spiritual wisdom

Our Mission

Science and Nonduality (SAND) is a community inspired by the timeless wisdom of nondual understanding and the revelations of cutting-edge science. We explore the nature of reality, consciousness, and human experience through conferences, retreats, and multimedia resources.

Founded in 2009, SAND brings together scientists, philosophers, spiritual teachers, artists, and seekers from around the world to explore the intersection of science and spirituality beyond conceptual frameworks.

"To explore beyond separate selves and limited views to the fundamental mystery of being."
